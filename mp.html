About MP

Madhya Pradesh is a state in central India, often referred to as the "Heart of India" due to its geographical location at the center of the country. It is the second-largest Indian state by area, covering 308,252 kmÂ² (119,017 sq mi), and the fifth-largest by population, with over 72.6 million residents as of the 2011 census. The state's capital is Bhopal, while Indore is its largest city. Madhya Pradesh shares borders with Rajasthan to the northwest, Uttar Pradesh to the northeast, Chhattisgarh to the east, Maharashtra to the south, and Gujarat to the west.
