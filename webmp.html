About MP

MP, or Madhya Pradesh, is India's second-largest state, known as the "Heart of India" due to its central location, rich biodiversity (Tiger State), UNESCO sites like Khajuraho & Sanchi . MP Tourism promotes Madhya Pradesh as a premier travel destination, highlighting its rich cultural heritage, wildlife, and natural beauty. It offers information on tourist attractions, accommodations, itineraries, and cultural experiences across the state. Notable initiatives include aerial tourism through the PM Shree Paryatan Helicopter Sewa, eco-tourism at Kuno National Park with the upcoming Kuno Forest Retreat & Festival, and immersive village homestays featuring traditional cuisine and folk arts. Recent developments include a new trek in Pench National Park and the 10th edition of GIFLIF Indiestaan in Bhopal, featuring international acts like Bloodywood. There are no customer reviews or ratings available for the tourism office itself, but its programs reflect a focus on adventure, sustainability, and cultural promotion
